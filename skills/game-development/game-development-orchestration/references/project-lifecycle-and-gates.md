# Project lifecycle and gates

Back to [Game Development Orchestration](../SKILL.md).

This self-contained playbook applies the verified synthesis in the game-development source register. Do not depend on or reopen the original book files during execution.

| Gate | Evidence | Recycle or stop when |
|---|---|---|
| Product thesis | player, repeated action, emotion, audience, platform, business constraint | audience is everyone or differentiation is only setting |
| Paper proof | rules and frequency-ranked interactions tested cheaply | rules remain disputed or presentation hides the question |
| Interaction proof | fresh-player comprehension and control evidence on real phones | core action remains unsatisfying after bounded iterations |
| Learning proof, when claimed | age/cohort, construct, comparator, measure, duration and transfer limit | only engagement or perceived learning was observed |
| Cultural permission | fact/fiction ledger, source provenance, named review scope, rights and unresolved disagreement | research is being treated as community permission |
| Foundation | clean build, data-driven tuning, source/asset conventions, save/resume | build depends on one machine or developer |
| Vertical slice | representative art, audio, code, controls, pipeline, cost and device profile | expensive systems are hidden or budgets fail |
| Production | releasable mainline, content throughput, regression and change control | feature growth breaks pillars, runway or pipeline |
| Alpha | feature complete and end-to-end completable | major mechanics are still being invented |
| Beta | compatibility, balance, localisation, accessibility and external testing | severe device/progression defects remain |
| Release candidate | reproducible signed artefact, provenance, store/legal/support/rollback | blockers, unknown rights or non-reproducible build remain |
| Sustain | triage, safe patches, postmortem and evidence-led roadmap | reactive updates break saves, economy or trust |

## Evidence-class ladder

Record the strongest evidence actually retained: `documented`, `prototype-observed`, `build-executed`, `device-measured`, `player-observed`, or `accountable-expert-approved`. These classes answer different questions and are not interchangeable. Every evidence item carries build/content identity, environment/cohort, method, result, failed path, limitation and owner.

## Non-intrusive advertising gate

An ad candidate must pass all of these before implementation or release:

- It is visibly an ad and cannot be confused with a game control, reward or system message.
- The player explicitly initiates it, or it occurs at a disclosed, genuine break after the requested action has completed; never during critical play, learning, narrative, save, recovery, safety, pause-to-stop or exit.
- Decline/close is obvious and does not remove earned value, create pay-to-win pressure or turn failure into monetised rescue.
- Placement, cap, pace, audience/age treatment, privacy purpose, creative rating, offline/no-fill/SDK-failure behaviour and removal switch are specified.
- The strictest current platform, territory, child/privacy and Chwezi rule applies. A legal/privacy owner approves applicability; a real-device test proves load/display/close/reward/recovery; player evidence checks interruption, regret and trust.

Fail closed to no ad. Revenue or retention cannot compensate for a safety, privacy, child, accessibility or trust failure.

Use one-question prototypes: assumption, population, device/context, success evidence, time/cost cap, and keep/change/cut/investigate disposition.
