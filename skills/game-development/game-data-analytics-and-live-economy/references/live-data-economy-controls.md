# Live data and economy controls

Every metric declares purpose, formula, population, time window, exclusions, event/schema versions, data latency, quality thresholds, uncertainty, owner, and decision. Every economy mutation declares source, sink, idempotency key, balance rule, fraud check, refund/reversal path, and audit event.

Remote changes require typed schema validation, environment separation, peer approval, exposure cohort, start/expiry, kill switch, telemetry guardrail, offline/default behaviour, and rollback rehearsal. A dashboard without a decision or a denominator is not evidence.

## Evidence and wellbeing pairing

Keep acquisition, engagement, learning, monetisation, wellbeing and trust as separate claim families. For an ad or economy experiment, retain build/config identity, audience/age/territory, placement, format, cap/pace, exposure denominator, consent state, data-quality result, revenue/cost basis, decline/close/accidental interaction, session exit, complaint/refund/regret and child-safety signals. Prespecify the decision and do not invent a benchmark.

An experiment fails when privacy, security, accessibility, child safety, cultural safety, ability to stop, harmful spend or trust crosses its stop condition, even if retention or revenue improves. Disable the affected placement/config through a tested kill switch and preserve the evidence and failed path.

Ads and Analytics are separate dependencies. Record each SDK/version, data fields, recipients, purpose, consent/opt-out, deletion, retention, offline behaviour, age designation and vendor exit plan. When a required control is missing, fail closed.
