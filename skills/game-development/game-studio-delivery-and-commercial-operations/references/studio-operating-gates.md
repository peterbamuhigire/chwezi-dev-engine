# Studio operating gates

A greenlight packet states player/product hypothesis, observed evidence, technical spikes, platform/compliance risks, content throughput, staffing gaps, cost/runway range, commercial path, stop conditions, and accountable approvers. A milestone cannot pass on percentage-complete reporting alone.

Estimates expose scope units, historical/benchmarked basis, assumptions, optimistic/likely/pessimistic range, integration and rework allowance, external dependencies, confidence, and re-estimation trigger. Vendor acceptance covers rights/provenance, editable source, naming/version, technical budgets, security transfer, defect remedy, and integration proof.

Portfolio and business-model comparisons include content throughput, rework, localisation, support, store/payment/tax, privacy/security, ratings, rights, vendor and live-operations costs. Record the source and date for each external assumption; do not invent demand, price, conversion, retention, fill, eCPM, revenue, cost, schedule or FIRE outcome.

Use `greenlight`, `narrow`, `pivot`, `hold` and `stop` thresholds tied to evidence. A commercial pass is subordinate to copyright, child safety, privacy, cultural permission, accessibility, security and wellbeing gates.

For advertising, accept only placements that pass the Chwezi non-intrusive contract and current platform/territory rules. Vendor acceptance additionally covers SDK data map, consent/deletion, age designation, creative review/reporting, cap/pace controls, offline/no-fill/failure behaviour, dependency removal, kill switch and real-device proof. Revenue projections remain scenarios until observed product data exists.
