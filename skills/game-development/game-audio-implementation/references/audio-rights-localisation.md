# Audio rights and localisation gates

Back to [Game Audio Implementation](../SKILL.md).

Record composer/performer, work, recording, consent, territory, term, media, edits, reuse, promotional/merchandise use, credits, fees/royalties, collecting-society status and cultural restrictions. Restricted or ambiguous material does not enter a build.

For voice, lock terminology and pronunciation, provide context/screenshot/emotion, externalise strings, time subtitles accessibly, handle plural/gender/name variation and test layout/audio fallback. Consult living-practice custodians before ritual, possession, praise or sacred repertoire representation.
