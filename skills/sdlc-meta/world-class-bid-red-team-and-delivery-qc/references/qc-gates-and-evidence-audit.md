# QC Gates and Evidence Audit

## Compliance Knockout Gate

Block release if any mandatory item is open:

- Eligibility missing or unverifiable.
- Required form missing or altered beyond allowed fields.
- Price appears in technical submission.
- Page, font, CV, file-size, language, or deadline rule fails.
- Required declaration cannot be signed truthfully.
- Submission structure does not match instructions.

## Evidence Audit

Every material claim needs:

- Claim text.
- Source or evidence.
- Source tier.
- Date verified.
- Relevance to claim.
- Confidentiality status.
- Whether it may appear in client/donor-facing output.

High-risk claims:

- Laws and regulatory status.
- Market size and growth figures.
- Payment, mobile money, logistics, and platform statistics.
- Unit economics, CAC, AOV, returns, commission, and margin figures.
- Team experience, reference project value, and country experience.

## Spreadsheet Review

- Inputs separated from formulas.
- Formula ranges correct.
- Totals reconcile to source schedules.
- VAT/tax treatment stated.
- Hidden rows/sheets checked.
- External links removed or justified.
- Workbook opens after export.

## Delivery Feasibility Review

Ask:

- Does the team have enough days for the promised work?
- Are approvals and data access realistic?
- Does the calendar fit reporting deadlines?
- Are expert roles backed by CV evidence?
- Are risks and mitigations concrete?

## Sign-Off Record

- Reviewer.
- Date.
- Artefact version.
- Critical findings.
- Fix status.
- Release decision.
